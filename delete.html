Data Deletion Instructions

If you want your data deleted from Social Automation App 2, follow these steps:

  1. Disconnect your social account from the application dashboard, if connected.
  2. Send an email to garjekaran5@gmail.com.
  3. In the email, include:
    • Your name
    • Your connected social account username
    • The platform name (for example: Threads, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X)
  4. We will review and process your data deletion request.
